Categories

A category describes the type of transaction (income, expense, tax, etc). It is used to generate reports and to fill in dialog fields when estimating taxes.

Category Types

  • Accrued Interest – Interest paid to the holder of a bond as part of the purchase price

  • Charity – Tax-deductible money given to a charity

  • Dividend – Dividend received from a stock or mutual fund

  • Estimated Federal Tax – Estimated federal tax payment

  • Estimated State Tax – Estimated state tax payment

  • Expense – Miscellaneous expense

  • Federal Tax – Federal tax payment or refund

  • Federal Tax-exempt Interest – Interest that is exempt from federal tax

  • Income – Miscellaneous income

  • Interest Expense – Expense related to generating interest income

  • Long-term Capital Gain – Gain or loss from a security held more than a year

  • Medical – Tax-deductible medical expense

  • Mortgage Interest – Interest on a mortgage

  • Pension – Pension income (1099-R)

  • Property Tax – Property tax payment

  • Short-term Capital Gain – Gain or loss from a security held less than a year

  • State Tax – State tax payment or refund

  • State Tax-exempt Interest – Interest that is exempt from state tax

  • Tax-exempt Interest – Interest that is exempt from both federal and state taxes

  • Taxable Interest – Interest that is subject to both federal and state taxes

  • Wages – Wage income (W-2)

Category Fields

  • Category Name – Identifies the category in the transaction dialogs and reports. The category name can be changed at any time.

  • Category Hidden – Hidden categories will not be displayed in the transaction dialog selection fields. Transactions with hidden categories will still be displayed.
